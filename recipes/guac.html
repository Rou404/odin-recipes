Fresh and Zesty Guacamole Recipe

Description:

Guacamole is a delicious and healthy dip that's perfect for parties or as a snack. This recipe delivers a zesty and creamy guacamole with a burst of flavor from fresh ingredients like avocados, tomatoes, and lime juice. Serve with tortilla chips or as a topping for your favorite Mexican dishes.

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe avocados, peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper (optional)

Steps:

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt. The lime juice not only adds flavor but prevents the guacamole from browning.
  2. Stir in the diced onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and minced garlic.
  3. Add a pinch of cayenne pepper for a hint of heat if desired.
  4. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 hour to let the flavors meld.
  5. Serve your fresh guacamole with tortilla chips or as a topping for tacos, burritos, or nachos.
  6. Enjoy the vibrant flavors of homemade guacamole!
